# Project History And Contributors

Mobility is developed mainly by [AREP](https://arep.fr) and [Elioth](https://elioth.com/) with [ADEME](https://wiki.resilience-territoire.ademe.fr/wiki/Mobility) support.

The project is open-source, and contributions are welcome. This page keeps a short record of people, schools, and projects that helped shape Mobility.

More information is also available on the [French Mobility website](https://mobility-team.github.io/).

## How To Contribute

Good first contributions include:

- testing the package on a small study area and reporting unclear steps,
- improving examples and documentation,
- looking at [good first issues](https://github.com/mobility-team/mobility/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22good+first+issue%22),
- opening an issue when the documentation does not explain what you needed.

If you use Mobility for research, teaching, or consulting, sharing the code behind your study helps other people understand and reproduce your work.
